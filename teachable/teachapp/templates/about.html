What is teachify ?

Teachify is a web-based tool that makes creating machine learning models fast, easy, and accessible to everyone.



How do I use it ?


1.Gather

Start by gathering the samples of the classes you want the model to learn, for this you can use your camera or you can simply upload the images from your computer.



2.Train

Train your model instantly using the gathered samples with a click of a button



3.Export

Once the training is done the model will be ready to be exported, you can download it and implement it in any other project
